Pasta Sauce

Description

Pasta is a quick italian meal made with tomato sauce, very quick and delicious meal

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of pasta
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 cup of tomatos, pepper, and fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of salt, 12 maggi
  • 2 tablespoon of oil

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot, add a little salt and cook the pasta untill soft little
  2. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  3. Add the oil in empty pot put the tomatos, pepper and fish sauce then the salt, and maggi.
  4. Add 4 cups of water inside the pot then the pasta
  5. Cover untill the pasta has dry in the pot
  6. Turns off the heat, serve hot and enjoy
