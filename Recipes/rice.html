Delicious Rice Recipes

Description

This simple rice recipesis fluffy, tasty, and perfect as a side dish or a base for any meal. its quick to prepare and goes well with stew, soup or vegetables

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of rice
  • 10 cups of water
  • 5 tomatos, peppe, onions, fish
  • 1 teaspoon of salt, 12 maggi
  • 2 tablespoon of oil

steps

  1. wash the rice throughly with clean water and put the rice in the pot with 5 cups of water to boil.
  2. ater the rice has boiled and its little soft then you will bring an empty pot again and put it in the fire.
  3. Add the oil in the empty pot put the grinded tomatos, peppe, onions and fish, then the salt, maggi and rice.
  4. Add 4 cups of water inside the pot
  5. Cover untill the rice has dry in the pot
  6. Turns off the heat, let it rest for five minutes, and serve
