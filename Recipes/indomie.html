INDOMIE NOODLES

Description

Indomie is a quick and tasty noodles dish loved by students and busy people. Its fast, spicy and statisfying!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of indomie
  • 1 cups of water
  • indomie seasoning
  • egg
  • vegetables and 1 teaspoon of oil

steps

  1. Boil water in a pot with egg inside.
  2. Add noodles and cook for minutes
  3. Add the oil, vegetables, seasoning and stir well
  4. Add 4 cups of water inside the pot
  5. Cover untill the indomie has dry in the pot
  6. Turns off the heat, serve and enjoy!
